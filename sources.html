YGO Paisano is a Yugioh youtuber that specializes in buying and selling cards daily, He owns a shop on tcgplayer, and does a marketwatch video every few days. He also analyzes trends in the current yugioh metagame in order to predict future prices.

House of Champs, or Jon Moore is a Yugioh youtuber that specializes in investing and making profit on yugioh. He gives advice on what cards to invest in, along with other yugioh goodies other youtubers have missed, such as figures, accessories, and other possible long-term investments.

mkohl40, or Robbie Kohl, is a Yugioh youtuber who specializes in the card market over in Japan, and how it translates to our market here in the US. Just as YGO Paisano, he closely follows the meta, and gives advice on investments for the future and what cards will go up and down later on down the line.
