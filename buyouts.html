Solemn Judgement $1.50

Solemn judgement is a good investment at the moment, being available for the low price of around $1.50. This card has always seen competitive play in yugioh, and with the new max rarity looking very nice, over time this card is bound to skyrocket in price.

Borrelsword Dragon $2.45

Borrelsword Dragon has been a staple of the Yugioh Boss Monsters for a few years now. It is very versatile, fitting into almost every extra deck regardless of what deck you're playing. In most meta decks, Borrelsword Dragon is a staple, and with the low price of around $2.50, it is a very good time to invest in this card, as we don't know when the next print of this card will be. Given that the new maximum gold printing has an alternate art as well, there is almost no way this card will not go up in price over the next few months.

Spirit Charmer Token $1.00

With the new Spirit Charmer structure deck that had just released, Konami released alongside it promotional tokens. Currently, they are locked behind the %10.00 paywall, as you can only obtain 1 random one when you buy a Spirit Charmer structure deck. While on the market these cards are sitting fairly low at around $1.00, it would be a good idea to invest in them, as token cards such as this tend to go up over time. Given that you cannot get them any other way, it would be wise to get a playset of each of the tokens before the collector market snatches them from the market.
